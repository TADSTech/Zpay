Correctness & trust

A hackathon build that behaves like production.

Every item on the Monnify production checklist — security, correctness, operations — is checked.

Signed webhooks

Every callback is verified with HMAC-SHA512 against the raw body before anything is trusted.

No double-charges

Idempotency keys bind every transaction, so repeats are ignored, not re-run.

Payment reconciliation

Over- and under-payments are caught automatically, with refunds triggered on excess.

Nightly reconciliation

A scheduled job compares our ledger to Monnify's records and flags any drift.

Secrets stay secret

Keys load from environment variables only — nothing sensitive is ever committed.

Health you can ping

A public /health endpoint reports live Monnify connectivity for judges to check.
